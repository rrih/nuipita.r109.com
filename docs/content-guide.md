# Content guide

Blog content is stored as typed local data in `src/content/blog/posts.ts`. Every article has a visible H1, description, date, reading time, category, structured blocks, and a clear path back to the product. Article copy explains measurement limits and uses “寸法から見た目安”; it does not promise fit, storage, safety, or durability.

Product screens do not link to `/blog`. Blog screens may link to the product. The policy test scans product source files to keep this direction one-way.
